How JARCADE Works

From zero to playing in four steps

No accounts required to play — though signing in saves your progress and favourites across devices.

1

Browse games

Explore curated rows on the home page or open Games for the full catalog, sorted into 13+ categories like Action, Racing and RPG.

2

Search instantly

Use the search bar in the header to filter games live by name — results update as you type.

3

View game details

Tap a card to see the game, rate it with a star, and add it to your favourites with the heart icon.

4

Play in your browser

Hit play and the game launches in an emulator right on the page — on phone or desktop.

Device Compatibility

Play anywhere

Most J2ME games are designed for a numeric keypad. JARCADE maps those controls to touch and keyboard for you.

Android & Mobile

Touch controls
  • An on-screen D-pad and action buttons appear over the game.
  • Rotate your device for landscape titles.
  • Tap fullscreen for the most immersive view.

Desktop & Laptop

Keyboard controls
  • Arrow keys move; Enter selects / fires.
  • Number keys map to the classic phone keypad (2,4,6,8).
  • Bigger screen, smoother performance.

Many games run on both — your controls switch automatically based on the device you're using.

Uploaded Games

Share your own games

Got a classic .jar to share? Publishing it takes under a minute.

1. Add your file

Open Upload Game and drag in a .jar file, or browse for it manually.

2. Add details

Give it a name, pick a category, write a short description, and optionally add a cover.

3. Find it under New

Once published it appears in the New row and inside its category.

Your uploads live in My Uploaded Games on the upload page, where you can manage or remove them anytime.

Troubleshooting Center

Fix it yourself

  1. Refresh the page — most launch hiccups clear with a reload.
  2. Update your browser to the latest version for full emulator support.
  3. Allow the page to finish loading — large games take a few seconds.
  4. Disable aggressive ad-blockers that may block the emulator scripts.
  1. Pick the right resolution when a game offers multiple sizes.
  2. Try fullscreen if controls feel cramped or off-screen.
  3. Restart the game from its page if it freezes mid-play.
  4. Report broken uploads via the Community section so we can review them.
  1. Create an account from the Login page to save data.
  2. Forgot your password? Use the reset link on the login screen.
  3. Favourites missing? Make sure you're signed in on the same account.
  4. Guest progress is stored on your device and won't sync between browsers.
  1. Use a modern browser — Chrome, Edge, Firefox or Safari.
  2. Rotate to landscape on mobile for wide games.
  3. Free up memory by closing other heavy tabs and apps.
  4. Check your connection — games load over the network the first time.
